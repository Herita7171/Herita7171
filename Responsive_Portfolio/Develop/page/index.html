About Me

Welcome to my page! My name is Hancong Wang. I'm a graduate student pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science at Northeastern University. Before studying Computer Science, I studied Economics and earned my Bachelor's degree at the University of Washington - Seattle.

I enjoy being challenged and engaging with projects that require me to work outside my comfort and knowledge set, as continuing to learn new knowledge and development techniques is important to my personal growth and success. Over the past school year, I designed and implemented a human-computer interactive Connect Four Game using Python. By applying minimax algorithm and alpha-beta pruning, I optimized the computer player by 20%. I also worked on a full-stack food delivery project with classmates, during which I designed and implemented REST APIs using Java Spark and learned some frameworks such as Spring.

In the upcoming semester, I'll be taking Database Management Systems and Fundamentals of Cloud Computing. I will also be the Teaching Assistant of the Object Oriented Design class. At the same time, I will continue learning front-end development and practice coding by developing some web applications.
